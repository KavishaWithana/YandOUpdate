The Story of Y&O.

Y & O Garments is a proudly Sri Lankan, family-owned apparel manufacturing company with a legacy of excellence, and craftsmanship.

Humble Beginnings

Founded by the late Mr. Sahabandu, the company has grown over the decades into a trusted name in quality garment production, employing over 10 individuals and supplying reliable products to the local and regional market.

Rooted in values of hard work, integrity, and empowerment, Y & O Garments combines traditional family values with a forward thinking approach to design, operations, and customer satisfaction. The company is currently directed by three generations of leadership: the founder’s wife, daughter, and grandson preserving the past while building for the future.

Our Journey Through the Years

2005

Company Founded

Founded in 2005, our company began as a small team working out of a garage with a vision to revolutionize the industry.

2008

First Major Contract

Secured our first major client, opening doors to new opportunities and growth.

2016

International Expansion

Opened our first international office in London, marking the beginning of our global presence.
